# PM-KISAN Deep Policy Intelligence Report

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi · Analytical Dissection & Policy Assessment

A single-file, interactive HTML report examining India's flagship farmer income support programme — covering design architecture, fiscal analysis, structural exclusions, implementation quality, global benchmarking, and evidence-based policy recommendations.

## Quick Facts

- Scheme: PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
- Launch: February 2019
- Type: Central Sector Scheme (100% Centre-funded)
- Benefit: ₹6,000/year per family in 3 installments of ₹2,000
- Coverage: ~11.5 crore farmer families (as of Mar 2026)
- Budget: ₹75,000 Cr/year (FY 2025–26 BE)
- Delivery: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) · Aadhaar-seeded
- Report Version: 2.0
- Last Verified: April 21, 2026

## Key Findings

### Strengths
- Unrivalled DBT scale: 11+ crore families, <5% leakage
- Consumption smoothing: Reduced distress borrowing in lean months
- Financial inclusion spillover: Accelerated Jan Dhan & Aadhaar-bank seeding
- Transparency: Public dashboard with village-level drilldown

### Structural Gaps
- Tenant farmer exclusion: ~30% of cultivators excluded due to land-title dependency
- Inflation erosion: ₹6,000 lost ~22–26% real value (2019–2026) without indexation
- Limited debt impact: Transfer insufficient against ~₹74,000 average household debt
- Inter-ministerial silos: No unified FarmerID across welfare schemes

### Overall Policy Score: 80/100 (B+ Grade)
- Coverage: 17/20 | Efficiency: 16/20 | Outcome: 14/20 | Transparency: 18/20 | Implementation: 15/20

## Priority Recommendations

Critical: Inflation-index transfers · Extend to tenant farmers via cultivator declaration · National Farmer Welfare ID
Medium: Grievance SLA mandate · Climate vulnerability supplement for high-risk zones
Long-Term: Unified convergence portal for PM-KISAN, PMFBY, KCC, PM-AASHA
